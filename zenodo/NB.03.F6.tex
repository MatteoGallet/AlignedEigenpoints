\section{Theorem}\label{theorem}}

    Let \(P_1, \dots, P_5\) be a \(V\)-configuration of points. Then
\(\mathrm{rk} \,\Phi(P_1, \dots, P_5) = 8\) if and only if one of the
following two conditions is satisfied: *
\(\delta_1(P_1, P_2, P_4) = 0\),
\(\overline{\delta}_1(P_1, P_2, P_3) = 0\),
